Identity & Device Safety System

Product Thinking, Compliance & User Retention

I had one user story I couldn't stop thinking about: a user loses their phone and someone picks it up. That question led to a strict one-device policy, an async KYC pipeline that keeps users off the critical path, and a PII architecture that gives sensitive data a single owner with one access path.

03

Fraud Risk & Profiling

Product Thinking & Engineering for Fraud Management

I noticed the fraud score was always computed at the moment a decision was needed — which meant the system only knew what it had time to check right then. I built something different: a service that watches continuously in the background, so by the time a transaction reaches execution, the risk score is already waiting.
